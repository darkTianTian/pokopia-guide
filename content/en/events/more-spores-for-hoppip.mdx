# More Spores for Hoppip

![More Spores for Hoppip event banner](/images/events/hoppip-event-hero.png)

Pokémon Pokopia's first limited-time event, **"More Spores for Hoppip"**, is live from **March 9 to March 24, 2026 (PDT)**. During this event, you can befriend three Pokémon that are **only available during this event window**: Hoppip, Skiploom, and Jumpluff.

## Event Details

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Event Name | More Spores for Hoppip |
| Start | March 9, 2026, 1:00 PM PDT |
| End | March 24, 2026, 12:59 PM PDT |
| Requirement | Must have rebuilt a Pokémon Center in your town |
| Cloud Island | Not available |

## What You Can Do

### Befriend Hoppip, Skiploom & Jumpluff

![Hoppip event gameplay screenshot](/images/events/hoppip-event-screenshot.png)

During the event period, Hoppip will appear in your town. By creating **picnic-themed habitats**, you can unlock Skiploom and Jumpluff as well.

> These three Pokémon species **cannot be encountered outside this event window**, making participation essential for collectors.

### Collect Cotton Spores

Special **cotton spores** will appear throughout your town during the event. Collect them and exchange them for **picnic-themed furniture items** to decorate your habitat.

## How to Participate

1. Make sure you have rebuilt a **Pokémon Center** in your town (approximately 30 minutes of gameplay needed)
2. Look for Hoppip appearing in your town after the event starts
3. Collect cotton spores scattered around your habitat
4. Exchange spores for picnic-themed furniture
5. Build picnic-themed habitats to attract Skiploom and Jumpluff

## Important Notes

- This event is **not available on Cloud Islands**
- You need approximately **30 minutes of gameplay** before the event content becomes accessible
- The early-purchase bonus **Ditto Rug** is still available via Mystery Gifts until January 31, 2027
