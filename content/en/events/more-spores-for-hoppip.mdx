# More Spores for Hoppip

![More Spores for Hoppip event banner](/images/events/hoppip-event-hero.png)

Pokémon Pokopia's first limited-time event, **"More Spores for Hoppip"**, is live from **March 9 to March 24, 2026 (PDT)**. During this event, you can befriend three Pokémon that are **only available during this event window**: Hoppip, Skiploom, and Jumpluff.

> Looking for timed research? Pokopia events work differently from Pokémon GO — instead of timed research tasks, you progress by collecting cotton spores and building specific habitats. Read on for the full step-by-step guide.

## Event Details

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Event Name | More Spores for Hoppip |
| Start | March 9, 2026, 1:00 PM PDT |
| End | March 24, 2026, 12:59 PM PDT |
| Requirement | Must have rebuilt a Pokémon Center in your town |
| Cloud Island | Not available |

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Talk to Hoppip at the Pokémon Center

After the event starts, head to your **Pokémon Center** (decorated with flowers during the event). You'll find Hoppip nearby — talk to it, and it will ask you to collect **cotton spores**.

### Step 2: Collect Cotton Spores on Dream Islands

Cotton spores spawn exclusively on **Dream Islands** during the event. To visit a Dream Island, give any doll to **Drifloon**. Collect as many spores as you can on each visit.

### Step 3: Exchange Spores for Picnic Furniture

Return to the **Pokémon Center** and trade your cotton spores for **picnic-themed furniture items**. These are the building blocks for the habitats you'll need in the next step.

### Step 4: Build Habitats to Attract Skiploom & Jumpluff

Use the furniture to build specific habitats:

| Habitat | Items Needed | Pokémon |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Field-Trip Friends | Flower Backpack, Lunch Box, Hoppip Water Bottle (2×2) | Skiploom |
| Dandelion Lunchtime | Dandy Flowers, Flower Cushion, Flowery Table Setting, Lunch Box (2×2) | Jumpluff |
| Yellow Carpet | 4× Dandy Flowers (2×2) | Hoppip, Skiploom & Jumpluff |

> These three Pokémon species **cannot be encountered outside this event window**, making participation essential for collectors. Note that Pokémon do not evolve in Pokopia — Hoppip, Skiploom, and Jumpluff must each be befriended separately through their own habitats.

## Important Notes

- This event is **not available on Cloud Islands**
- You need approximately **30 minutes of gameplay** before the event content becomes accessible (Pokémon Center must be rebuilt)
- The early-purchase bonus **Ditto Rug** is still available via Mystery Gifts until January 31, 2027
