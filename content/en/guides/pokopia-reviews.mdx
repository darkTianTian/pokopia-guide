# Pokémon Pokopia Review Roundup

Reviews for Pokémon Pokopia are in, and the Metacritic score stands at an impressive **90**, earning the "Universal Acclaim" rating and the coveted **Must-Play** badge. This makes Pokopia not only the **highest-rated game of 2026 so far**, but also the **highest-rated Pokémon game of all time** on Metacritic.

No Pokémon game — mainline or spin-off — had ever broken the 90 barrier on Metacritic before. Pokopia has changed that.

## What Critics Are Saying

### Praise

**IGN (9/10)** called it "a real treat: an enjoyable building and town simulator that capitalizes on the charming personalities of its monsters, striking a healthy balance between freedom and suggestion in its building mechanics."

**Dexerto (10/10)** said "Pokopia reimagines what it means to be a Pokémon, blending cozy building systems with open-ended creativity to deliver one of the most addictive gameplay loops the series has ever seen."

**VGC (5/5)** described it as "Minecraft and Animal Crossing combine in Pokémon's best spin-off."

**Kotaku** called it "a brilliantly bizarre blend of Pokémon, Animal Crossing, Dragon Quest Builders, and Viva Piñata that despite its many influences manages to be something uniquely its own."

### Criticism

Not every review was glowing. **The Gamer (3/5)** felt that the building quests can be quite repetitive at times. However, only 2 reviews on Metacritic were classified as "Mixed."

## Common Themes Across Reviews

Several keywords appeared frequently across critic reviews:

- **Cozy & relaxing** — The slow-life sandbox delivers a soothing core experience
- **Creative freedom** — The building system offers impressive flexibility
- **Pokémon personality** — Each Pokémon has distinct character and interactions
- **Content-rich** — Crafting recipes, cooking, music, multiplayer modes
- **Cloud Island** — The async multiplayer island system was universally praised

## Should You Buy It?

Based on the critical consensus:

- **If you enjoy** Animal Crossing, Stardew Valley, or Minecraft → **Highly recommended**
- **If you're a** Pokémon fan looking for a fresh take on the series → **Highly recommended**
- **If you only want** traditional Pokémon battling and collecting → **Wait and see** — this is not a traditional RPG
