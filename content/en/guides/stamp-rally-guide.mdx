# Pokopia Stamp Rally Guide: How to Earn Max Life Coins

The Stamp Rally is a weekly collectible activity in Pokémon Pokopia that rewards you with Life Coins. Collect stamps from PCs around the world, fill your stamp card, and exchange it every Friday for coins. Rarer stamps earn significantly more — this guide covers how to maximize your weekly payout.

## How to Unlock the Stamp Rally

The Stamp Rally unlocks once you gain access to a PC (Personal Computer) in any Pokémon Center. You do **not** need to repair a Pokémon Center to use its PC — any PC on the map works, even in areas you haven't fully restored.

## How It Works

1. Pick up a **stamp card** from any PC
2. Interact with a PC **once per day** to press one stamp
3. Fill your card with **5 stamps** throughout the week (Monday–Friday)
4. **Every Friday**, exchange your completed card for **Life Coins**

## Stamp Types and Rewards

There are two types of stamps:

| Stamp Type | Description | Coins per Stamp |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| Regular | Common Pokémon stamps | ~400 |
| Legendary | Rare legendary Pokémon stamps | ~725 |

### Present Box Spaces

Your stamp card has **present box marks** on **Day 1, Day 3, and Day 5**. When a stamp lands on a present box space, you earn a **bonus of ~650 Life Coins** per present box.

### Total Rewards

- A regular 5-stamp card earns approximately **2,000 Life Coins**
- Filling present box spaces and collecting legendary stamps can push your total significantly higher

> Legendary stamps and present box bonuses are the key to maximizing your weekly Life Coins. Always aim to place the best stamps on present box spaces.

## Where to Collect Stamps

You can collect stamps from PCs in three types of locations:

### Your Own Island

- One stamp per day from any PC on your island
- The stamp is the **same** regardless of which PC you use
- Stamp type is randomized daily

### Friends' Islands (Multiplayer)

- Visit a friend's world and use their PC for a **different stamp**
- This is the best way to collect multiple stamps per day
- Each friend's world may offer a different stamp

### Cloud Islands

- Different Cloud Islands can have **different stamps**
- Check multiple Cloud Islands daily for better stamps
- Great for finding rare legendary Pokémon stamps

## How to Swap Stamps

After filling your card with 5 stamps, you can still collect new stamps and **swap** them with existing ones on your card. This is the key to maximizing your rewards:

1. Fill your card with any 5 stamps first
2. Continue collecting stamps from friends and Cloud Islands
3. Replace regular stamps with **legendary stamps**
4. Prioritize placing the best stamps on **present box spaces** (Day 1, 3, 5)
5. Exchange your card on Friday when you're satisfied

## Tips for Maximizing Life Coins

- **Visit friends daily** — multiplayer PCs are your best source of legendary stamps
- **Check multiple Cloud Islands** — each one may have different stamps
- **Don't rush to exchange** — keep swapping until you get the best stamps possible
- **Target present box spaces** — Day 1, 3, and 5 slots give bonus coins
- **Play every day** — stamps reset daily, so consistency is key
- **Coordinate with friends** — share which worlds have legendary stamps that day
- **Fill your card early** — this gives you more days to swap for upgrades
