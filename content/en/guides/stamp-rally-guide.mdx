# Pokopia Stamp Rally Guide: How to Earn Max Life Coins

The Stamp Rally is a weekly collectible activity in Pokémon Pokopia that rewards you with Life Coins. By collecting stamps from PCs around the world, you can fill stamp cards and exchange them for coins — with rarer stamps worth significantly more. This guide covers everything you need to maximize your weekly earnings.

## How to Unlock the Stamp Rally

The Stamp Rally unlocks once you gain access to a PC (Personal Computer) in any Pokémon Center. You do **not** need to repair a Pokémon Center to use its PC — any PC on the map works, even in areas you haven't fully restored.

## How It Works

1. **Every Friday**, a new stamp card appears at any PC
2. Interact with a PC each day to receive one stamp
3. Fill your card with **5 stamps** throughout the week
4. Exchange your completed card for **Life Coins**

## Stamp Rarity

Stamps come in 4 rarity tiers, each with a color-coded border:

| Rarity | Border Color | Drop Rate | Coins per Stamp |
|--------|-------------|-----------|-----------------|
| Common | Green | ~70% | Low |
| Uncommon | Blue | ~20% | Medium |
| Rare | Purple | ~5% | High |
| Super Rare | Gold | <5% | ~300 |

A card filled entirely with **Gold stamps** earns up to approximately **1,500 Life Coins** per week.

> The rarity of your stamps directly determines your Life Coin payout. Always aim for the highest rarity possible.

## Where to Collect Stamps

You can collect stamps from PCs in three types of locations:

### Your Own Island

- One stamp per day from any PC on your island
- The stamp is the **same** regardless of which PC you use
- Rarity is randomized daily

### Friends' Islands (Multiplayer)

- Visit a friend's world and use their PC for a **different stamp**
- This is the best way to collect multiple stamps per day
- Each friend's world may offer a different rarity

### Cloud Islands

- Different Cloud Islands can have **different stamps**
- Check multiple Cloud Islands daily for better rarity
- Great for finding rare Purple and Gold stamps

## How to Swap Stamps

After filling your card with 5 stamps, you can still collect new stamps and **swap** them with existing ones on your card. This is key to maximizing your rewards:

1. Fill your card with any 5 stamps first
2. Continue collecting stamps from friends and Cloud Islands
3. Replace lower-rarity stamps (Green/Blue) with higher ones (Purple/Gold)
4. Exchange your card when you're satisfied with the rarity

## Tips for Maximizing Life Coins

- **Visit friends daily** — multiplayer PCs are your best source of rare stamps
- **Check multiple Cloud Islands** — each one may have different stamp rarities
- **Don't rush to exchange** — keep swapping until you get the best stamps possible
- **Play every day** — stamps reset daily, so consistency is key
- **Coordinate with friends** — share which worlds have rare stamps that day
- **Fill your card early** — this gives you more days to swap for upgrades
