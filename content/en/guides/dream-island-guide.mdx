# Pokopia Dream Island Guide: All Dolls, Islands & Legendary Pokémon

Dream Islands are special uninhabited islands in Pokémon Pokopia filled with rare materials, Stardust, and even legendary Pokémon. By showing dolls to Drifloon, you can visit a different island each day to farm resources. This guide covers every doll location, island type, and what you can find on each.

## How Dream Islands Work

1. Find a **Pokémon Doll** while exploring
2. Show the doll to **Drifloon** (or examine it nearby)
3. Drifloon takes you to the corresponding **Dream Island**
4. Explore, collect materials, and check underground caves for legendaries
5. Return home with everything you gathered

**Daily rules:**
- You can only visit **one Dream Island per day**
- Resources **respawn daily** — visit again tomorrow for more
- Resets at **5:00 AM**
- You can revisit the same island multiple times in one day, but items won't respawn until the next day

## Legendary Pokémon on Dream Islands

Each Dream Island (except Rock Peak) has a chance to spawn a legendary Pokémon in the **underground caves**. When you arrive, look for **2 cave entrances** near the central area.

| Island | Legendary |
|--------|-----------|
| Wasteland | [Suicune](/pokedex/suicune) |
| Ocean | [Raikou](/pokedex/raikou) |
| Volcanic | [Entei](/pokedex/entei) |
| Sky | [Mewtwo](/pokedex/mewtwo) |

> **Important:** [Raikou](/pokedex/raikou) and [Mewtwo](/pokedex/mewtwo) may require prior story encounters before they can appear on Dream Islands. If they don't spawn, progress further in the main story first.

**If no legendary appears:** Return to Drifloon and try again the next day. Legendary spawns are random with a low probability.

## Stardust & Star Fragments

All Dream Islands contain **Stardust** scattered along coastlines and paths. Collecting **99 Stardust** lets you craft a **Star Fragment**, which is valuable for multiplayer gifting and trading.

> **Tip:** Pick up every Stardust you see — it adds up over time and Star Fragments are one of the best multiplayer gifts.

## Other Finds on Dream Islands

- **CDs** — collectible music tracks
- **Notebooks** — hidden lore about Pokopia's world
- **Habitat Tips** — hints for habitats you haven't discovered yet
- **Outfits** — character-themed clothing (Blue, Green, Ethan)

## Tips for Dream Island Farming

1. **Visit every day** — daily visits are the most consistent way to stock rare materials
2. **Prioritize based on needs** — farming [Concrete](/guides/all-crafting-materials)? Visit Rock Peak for Limestone. Need [Bricks](/guides/all-crafting-materials)? Ocean Island has Twine
3. **Always check underground caves** — legendary Pokémon only appear there
4. **Collect all Stardust** — 99 Stardust → 1 Star Fragment for multiplayer
5. **Find all 7 dolls** — more dolls = more island options each day
6. **Use Search Pokémon** — they help locate buried dolls and hidden items
