# Pokémon Pokopia Pre-Order Guide

Pokémon Pokopia is the first slow-life sandbox game in the Pokémon series, launching worldwide on **March 5, 2026** exclusively for **Nintendo Switch 2**.

This guide covers all pre-order channels, regional pricing, and retailer-exclusive bonuses to help you choose the best way to purchase.

## Game Overview

- **Title**: Pokémon Pokopia / ぽこ あ ポケモン
- **Release Date**: March 5, 2026 (worldwide)
- **Platform**: Nintendo Switch 2
- **Genre**: Slow-life sandbox
- **Players**: 1–4 (online multiplayer supported)
- **Physical Edition Note**: The physical version is a "Key Card" that requires an internet connection to download on first play, with 10GB of free storage needed

## Regional Pricing

| Region | Price |
|--------|-------|
| United States | $69.99 |
| Japan | ¥8,980 (tax included) |
| United Kingdom | £49.99 |

> Digital and physical versions are priced the same. The digital version unlocks at midnight on March 5, so you can play without waiting for delivery.

## Universal Early Purchase Bonus

All purchases include the in-game **Ditto Rug** decoration, redeemable via the Mystery Gift feature. The redemption deadline is **January 31, 2027**.

This item can also be obtained through normal gameplay later, so there's no need to worry about missing it.
