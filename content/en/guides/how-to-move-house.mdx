# How to Move House in Pokémon Pokopia

Built a house in the wrong spot? No need to demolish it and waste materials. Pokémon Pokopia lets you relocate buildings using a Relocation Kit, and you can change which building is your home at any time with a Ditto Flag.

## How to Move Small Buildings

For small structures, you can pick them up directly:

1. Walk up to the building and face it
2. Hold down the **A button**
3. Your character will lift the building
4. Carry it to the desired location and place it down

This only works for small player-built structures like basic Pokémon houses.

## How to Move Large Buildings with a Relocation Kit

Larger structures require a ![icon:Relocation Kit](/images/items/item-322.png) **Relocation Kit**. This is the safest way to move buildings without losing materials.

### Steps:

1. Craft or purchase a **Relocation Kit**
2. Place it directly in front of the building you want to move
3. Assign a Pokémon with the ![icon:Bulldoze](/images/specialties/bulldoze.png) **Bulldoze** specialty to the kit
4. The Pokémon will prepare the building for relocation
5. Choose the new location to place the building

> **Important:** Only player-built structures can be relocated. Pre-existing world structures like the lighthouse cannot be moved.

## Relocation Kit vs. Demolition Kit

| Item | What It Does | Materials Lost? |
|------|-------------|-----------------|
| ![icon:Relocation Kit](/images/items/item-322.png) Relocation Kit | Moves the building to a new spot | No |
| ![icon:Demolition Kit](/images/items/item-323.png) Demolition Kit | Destroys the building entirely | Yes |

Always use a Relocation Kit if you just want to reposition a building. Only use a Demolition Kit if you want to remove a structure completely.

## How to Set Your Home with a Ditto Flag

Your "home" in Pokopia is whichever building has a ![icon:Ditto Flag](/images/items/item-42.png) **Ditto Flag** placed outside it. This determines your **Return Home** fast travel destination.

### Crafting a Ditto Flag

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| ![icon:Sturdy Stick](/images/items/item-4.png) Sturdy Stick | 1 |
| ![icon:Twine](/images/items/item-52.png) Twine | 1 |
| Leppa Berry | 1 |

### How to Use It:

1. Craft the Ditto Flag at a crafting station
2. Place it outside any Pokémon house you've built
3. That building becomes your designated home

To change your home, simply pick up the Ditto Flag and place it outside a different building.

## Return Home Fast Travel

Once you've set a home with a Ditto Flag, you can fast travel back to it at any time:

1. Open the menu
2. Select **Return Home**
3. You'll be teleported to your designated home

This is especially useful when exploring far from your base or after gathering resources in remote areas.

## Quick Tips

1. **Always relocate, never demolish** — Use Relocation Kits to save materials when repositioning buildings
2. **Plan your layout early** — Think about where you want different habitat zones before building
3. **Get a Bulldoze Pokémon early** — You'll need one for Relocation Kits and terrain work
4. **Move your Ditto Flag as you expand** — Place it at whatever base you're using most often
5. **World structures are fixed** — Don't waste time trying to move the lighthouse or other pre-built landmarks
