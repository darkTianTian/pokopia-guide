# Pokopia Beginner's Guide

Welcome to the world of Pokopia! This guide will help you quickly understand the core mechanics of the game and get your adventure off to a great start.

## Choose Your Starter Companion

At the beginning of the game, you can choose one of three Pokémon as your starter companion:

- **Fire-type** — Great for trainers who love offense
- **Water-type** — A balanced choice for attack and defense
- **Grass-type** — Excels at support and endurance battles

> Tip: No matter which starter Pokémon you choose, it won't affect your later gameplay experience. Just pick your favorite!

## Battle System Basics

Pokopia uses a turn-based battle system. Each turn you can:

1. **Use a Move** — Each Pokémon can carry up to 4 moves
2. **Switch Pokémon** — Flexibly switch based on type matchups
3. **Use Items** — Restore HP, cure status conditions, etc.
4. **Run Away** — Only available in wild Pokémon battles

### Type Effectiveness

Type effectiveness is the core of battles. For example:

| Attack Type | Super Effective | Not Very Effective |
|------------|----------------|-------------------|
| Fire | Grass, Ice, Bug, Steel | Water, Fire, Rock, Dragon |
| Water | Fire, Ground, Rock | Water, Grass, Dragon |
| Grass | Water, Ground, Rock | Fire, Grass, Poison, Flying, Bug, Dragon, Steel |

## Explore the Pokopia World

Pokopia has rich regions waiting for you to explore:

- **Emerald Plains** — Starting area, great for leveling up
- **Azure Coast** — A paradise for Water-type Pokémon
- **Blaze Volcano** — High-level challenge area
- **Starlight Forest** — Home to rare Pokémon

## Next Steps

After mastering the basics, we recommend:

1. Catch a few Pokémon of different types in Emerald Plains
2. Build a team with balanced type coverage
3. Challenge the first Gym
4. Check out our advanced guides for more tips
