# How to Get Lumber in Pokémon Pokopia

Lumber is one of the most important [crafting](/crafting) materials in Pokémon Pokopia, but it's significantly more scarce than Stone. You'll need it to build the Pokémon Center, craft [furniture](/items) like Storage Boxes, Log Tables, and more. This guide covers every method to get Lumber and how to maximize your supply.

![Ditto holding lumber in Pokopia](/images/guides/pokopia-ditto-holding-lumber.webp)

## Method 1: Pick Up Lumber from the Ground

The simplest way to get Lumber is to find it scattered across the map. Look for tiny fragments of broken wood on the ground and press **Y** to pick them up. Each piece equals one unit of Lumber.

**Best locations in the Withered Wasteland (first area):**

- Dry plains to the right of the entrance
- Higher ground to the left
- Cave beyond the elevated terrain

![Finding broken lumber on the ground](/images/guides/pokopia-finding-lumber.webp)

You may also find neatly stacked piles of lumber — these yield the same amount as scattered fragments.

![Stacked lumber piles](/images/guides/pokopia-stacked-lumber.webp)

> This method is slow. For large quantities, use the Chop method below.

## Method 2: Convert Small Logs into Lumber (Recommended)

This is the most efficient way to get Lumber. The process works in two steps:

### Step 1: Get Small Logs

Small Logs are the raw material for making Lumber. You can obtain them by:

- ![icon:Cut](/images/cooking/moves/cut.png) **Cutting down trees** — Use the Cut move on trees and worn lumber scattered throughout the Withered Wasteland
- **Receiving gifts** from Pokémon in the area
- **Checking sparkling water ripples** near beaches

### Step 2: Give Logs to a ![icon:Chop](/images/specialties/chop.png) Chop Pokémon

Find a Pokémon with the ![icon:Chop](/images/specialties/chop.png) **Chop** specialty, speak to it and select **"Look at this"**, then pick Small Logs from your inventory.

![Scyther processing small logs into lumber](/images/guides/pokopia-scyther-making-lumber.webp)

**Conversion rate:** 10 Small Logs → **50 Lumber**

You can give a maximum of 10 Small Logs at once. This is significantly more efficient than making Bricks or Paper through similar processes.

> **Tip:** If you speak to the Pokémon while it's still working, it will give you whatever Lumber it has already finished chopping. If you wait until it's done, the Pokémon will come find you to deliver the rest.

## Pokémon with ![icon:Chop](/images/specialties/chop.png) Chop Specialty

The three Pokémon with the ![icon:Chop](/images/specialties/chop.png) Chop specialty available in the Withered Wasteland (first area) are:

| Pokémon | Type | Specialties | How to Find |
|---------|------|-------------|-------------|
| [Scyther](/pokedex/scyther) | Bug / Flying | ![icon:Chop](/images/specialties/chop.png) Chop | Create a [Tree-Shaded Tall Grass](/habitat/tree-shaded-tall-grass) habitat |
| [Pinsir](/pokedex/pinsir) | Bug | ![icon:Chop](/images/specialties/chop.png) Chop, ![icon:Build](/images/specialties/build.png) Build | Create a [Tree-Shaded Tall Grass](/habitat/tree-shaded-tall-grass) habitat |
| [Heracross](/pokedex/heracross) | Bug / Fighting | ![icon:Chop](/images/specialties/chop.png) Chop, ![icon:Build](/images/specialties/build.png) Build | Create a [Tree-Shaded Tall Grass](/habitat/tree-shaded-tall-grass) habitat |

### How to Create a Tree-Shaded Tall Grass Habitat

To attract these Pokémon, place **4 Tall Grass patches** around any **Large Tree** in the Withered Wasteland. This creates the [Tree-Shaded Tall Grass](/habitat/tree-shaded-tall-grass) [habitat](/habitat), which will draw in Scyther, Pinsir, and other Pokémon.

## What Is Lumber Used For?

Lumber is a core [crafting](/crafting) material required for many items:

| Item | Materials |
|------|-----------|
| Storage Box | Lumber x1 |
| Log Table | Small Log x1 |
| Log Chair | Small Log x1 |
| Pokémon Center (rebuild) | Lumber x20, Rock x20, Leaf x10, Vine Rope x10 |

> For more crafting recipes, check the [All Crafting Materials guide](/guides/all-crafting-materials) and [Crafting page](/crafting).

## Quick Tips for Getting Lumber Fast

1. **Set up the [Tree-Shaded Tall Grass](/habitat/tree-shaded-tall-grass) [habitat](/habitat) early** — unlock ![icon:Chop](/images/specialties/chop.png) Chop Pokémon as soon as possible
2. **Stock up on Small Logs** before visiting your Chop Pokémon — bring 10 at a time for maximum efficiency
3. **Learn the ![icon:Cut](/images/cooking/moves/cut.png) Cut move** to access worn lumber piles in the Withered Wasteland
4. **Check your Chop Pokémon frequently** — you can collect partial batches while they're still working
5. **Explore thoroughly** — Lumber fragments appear in caves and elevated areas, not just open fields
