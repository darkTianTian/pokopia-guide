# How to Get Onix in Pokémon Pokopia

Onix is one of the most important Pokémon in Pokopia — its **Bulldoze** specialty is essential for rebuilding the Pokémon Center and relocating buildings. You'll unlock Onix by completing the **"Yawn Up A Storm"** main story quest in the Withered Wasteland.

## Where to Find Onix

Onix is trapped in a cave **northwest of the Withered Wasteland Pokémon Center**, near the gate. You can reach the cave two ways:

- Use **Rock Smash** on the ground diagonally from Scyther's tree to reveal a cave gap
- Destroy blocks on the nearby road to reach the beach, which has another cave entrance

Follow the speech bubbles inside until you hear someone calling for help. You'll find Onix pinned behind unbreakable rocks.

> **Don't try to smash the rocks yet** — they cannot be broken until rain softens them. This is the most common mistake players make.

## Step-by-Step: Freeing Onix

### Step 1: Talk to Professor Tangrowth

After discovering Onix, return to **Professor Tangrowth**. Escort them to the beach ruins for a group meeting with your Pokémon. The conclusion: you need Slowpoke to make it rain.

### Step 2: Find and Attract Slowpoke

Build a **Seaside Tall Grass** habitat to attract Slowpoke:

- Place **at least 4 Tall Grass blocks adjacent to the ocean**
- Wait for Slowpoke to appear

### Step 3: Guide Slowpoke to the Pokémon Center

Slowpoke moves slowly and cannot jump. You need to:

- Build **stairs with varying heights** for Slowpoke to navigate terrain
- Soak any **cracked dry ground** with Water Gun before Slowpoke crosses it
- Talk to Slowpoke if it stops moving — it will tell you what's blocking its path

### Step 4: Raise Humidity

Slowpoke won't yawn unless the environment is humid enough. You'll see the message: *"Slowpoke tells you that it's too dry, so they're feeling very awake."*

Methods to raise humidity:

- **Water dry ground** with Water specialty Pokémon
- **Plant seeds and flowers** (Bulbasaur can help with Grow)
- **Break brown blocks** plugging old water sources with Rock Smash
- **Place a Horsea Fountain** (50 Coins from the PC Shop)
- **Add decorative fountains and sprinklers**

> For a detailed guide, see [How to Raise Humidity in Pokémon Pokopia](/guides/how-to-raise-humidity).

### Step 5: Set Up a Rain Dance Site

Follow Squirtle's guidance to set up a Rain Dance Site:

| Item | How to Get |
|------|-----------|
| 2 Castform Weather Charms (Rain Form) | 1 from the shop, others in the cave |
| 1 Plate of Food | Available at the shop |
| Leppa Berries | Found in trees throughout the area |

Assign a **Water specialty** Pokémon to perform the Rain Dance.

### Step 6: Trigger Rain and Free Onix

Once humidity is high enough:

1. Interact with Slowpoke to trigger its **Yawn** — rain begins
2. If rain stops, talk to **Kyogre** at the ocean to start a second round
3. Return to Onix's cave — the ground has softened
4. Use **Rock Smash** to break the clay bricks blocking Onix
5. You only need to break enough blocks — Onix handles the rest

## Building Onix's Habitat

After the rescue, build a **Smooth Tall Grass** habitat for Onix:

1. Find the **Smooth Rock** — it's in the same cave where you freed Onix, on a grey block on the left wall before the path branches
2. Create **Dry Tall Grass** — use **Leafage on unwatered dirt** (this is the only way; it cannot be bought)
3. Arrange a **2×2 square of Dry Tall Grass** and place the Smooth Rock next to it
4. The game confirms: *"Restored a Smooth Tall Grass habitat"*
5. Press the **up directional button** near Onix to guide it to its new home

> **Don't miss the Smooth Rock** before leaving the cave — go back for it if you forgot.

## Why Onix Is Essential

Onix's specialties make it one of the most valuable Pokémon in the Withered Wasteland:

| Specialty | What It Does |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Bulldoze** | Required for rebuilding the Pokémon Center, operating Relocation Kits, and demolition tasks |
| **Crush** | Crushes fruits into paint for customization |

### Rebuilding the Pokémon Center

Onix is **mandatory** for the Pokémon Center rebuild. You need:

- **Rebuilding Kit** — 1,000 Life Coins from the PC Shop
- **8 Pokémon assistants** with specific specialties (Onix must be one of them)
- **Materials:** 20 Lumber, 20 Stone, 10 Leaf, 10 Vine Rope

## Quick Tips

1. **Don't rush to the cave** — Focus on raising humidity first; you can't free Onix without rain
2. **Clear Slowpoke's entire path in advance** — Water Gun every dry patch before escorting it
3. **Grab the Smooth Rock from the cave** — Don't leave without it or you'll have to go back
4. **Dry Tall Grass needs Leafage on dry dirt** — Don't waste time looking for it in shops
5. **Leave space around the habitat** — Onix is large and needs room to navigate to its home
