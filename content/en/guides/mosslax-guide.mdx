# Pokopia Mosslax Guide: All Offering Buffs & Best Food

Mosslax is a moss-covered [Snorlax](/pokedex/snorlax) sleeping in your town in Pokémon Pokopia. Once awakened, you can offer it food daily to receive powerful buff effects that last until 5:00 AM the next day. Mosslax has the ![icon:Eat](/images/specialties/eat.png) **Eat** specialty — this guide covers every flavor buff, the best offerings, and how to maximize your benefits.

## How to Unlock Mosslax Offerings

1. Progress through the **Bleak Beach** story
2. Complete the quest **"Brighten Things Up!"** (make the dark streets bright)
3. Mosslax will awaken and a **plate** will appear in front of it
4. Place food on the plate once per day to receive a buff

## All Offering Buffs by Flavor

The buff you receive depends on the **dominant flavor** of the food you offer. There are 6 flavor types:

| Flavor | Effect | Best For |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **No Flavor** | Grow closer to Pokémon faster | Befriending new Pokémon |
| **Spicy** | Pokémon appear more often in [habitats](/habitat) | [Pokédex](/pokedex) completion, raising environment level |
| **Dry** | More likely to see Pokémon with rare feathers ([Ho-Oh](/pokedex/ho-oh), [Lugia](/pokedex/lugia)) | Obtaining ![icon:Rainbow Feather](/images/items/item-31.png) Rainbow Feather & ![icon:Silver Feather](/images/items/item-203.png) Silver Feather |
| **Sweet** | Easier to find ancient artifacts and lost items | Collecting fossils, finding ![icon:Mysterious Slate](/images/items/item-226.png) Mysterious Slates (for [Mew](/pokedex/mew)) |
| **Bitter** | More likely to find rare items while exploring | Resource farming |
| **Sour** | Shop more likely to stock good items | Shopping for rare goods |

## Best Offerings Recommended

### For Legendary Pokémon Hunters: Dry Flavor

Offer ![icon:Mushroom Soup](/images/cooking/recipes/mushroom-soup.png) **Mushroom Soup** or ![icon:Mushroom Hamburger](/images/cooking/recipes/mushroom-hamburger.png) **Mushroom Hamburger Steak** (dry flavor) to increase the chance of [Ho-Oh](/pokedex/ho-oh) and [Lugia](/pokedex/lugia) appearing. You need their feathers to craft the ![icon:Clear Bell](/images/items/item-372.png) Clear Bell and ![icon:Tidal Bell](/images/items/item-355.png) Tidal Bell.

### For Pokédex Completion: Spicy Flavor

Offer ![icon:Herbal Soup](/images/cooking/recipes/herbal-soup.png) **Herbal Soup** or ![icon:Crouton Salad](/images/cooking/recipes/crouton-salad.png) **Crouton Salad** (spicy flavor) to boost Pokémon spawn rates in your [habitats](/habitat). This is the best daily-use buff for most players.

### For Finding Mew: Sweet Flavor

Offer ![icon:Potato Hamburger](/images/cooking/recipes/potato-hamburger.png) **Potato Hamburger Steak** (sweet flavor) to increase ![icon:Mysterious Slate](/images/items/item-226.png) Mysterious Slate drop rates in Withered Wasteland. You need all 27 slates to unlock [Mew](/pokedex/mew).

## Cooked vs Raw: Does It Matter?

**Yes!** Cooked dishes provide **stronger buff effects** than raw ingredients. Always offer a [cooked meal](/cooking) rather than a raw apple or potato for maximum benefit.

For example:
- Offering a raw ingredient gives a **basic** buff
- Offering a cooked dish of the same flavor gives an **enhanced** buff

Check our [Cooking Recipes](/cooking) page for all recipes organized by flavor.

## Important Rules

- **Once per day** — you can only offer food to Mosslax once per in-game day
- **Buff lasts until 5:00 AM** — effects persist through the entire day once activated
- **Cannot be changed** — once you offer food, you're locked into that buff until the next day
- **Don't switch mid-day** — pick your flavor and commit to it

## Recommended Daily Routine

1. **Check your goals** — Do you need feathers? Pokémon spawns? Rare items?
2. **Cook the right dish** — Match the flavor to your goal (see [Cooking Recipes](/cooking))
3. **Offer to Mosslax** — Place the cooked food on the plate
4. **Play the rest of the day** — The buff will be active until 5:00 AM tomorrow

> **Tip:** Combine Mosslax buffs with [Dream Island](/guides/dream-island-guide) visits for maximum daily efficiency. Use Dry flavor when farming feathers, then visit Dream Islands for additional rare materials.
